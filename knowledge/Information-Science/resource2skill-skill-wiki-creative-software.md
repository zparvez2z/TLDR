# 1 Resource2Skill distills multimodal resources into a hierarchical Skill Wiki across seven creative software domains.

This work introduces Resource2Skill, a pipeline that converts multimodal learning resources (e.g., tutorials, documentation, and videos) into a structured, hierarchical Skill Wiki for creative software. The system organizes skills into a taxonomy that captures tasks, sub-skills, and tool-specific techniques across seven creative software domains. By leveraging large language models and multimodal parsing, it standardizes unstructured content into machine- and human-readable knowledge assets for training, assistance, and curriculum design. The authors report broad coverage and practical utility, indicating potential for improved skill discovery, personalized learning, and tool-augmented assistance.
- Distills text and video tutorials into a hierarchical skill taxonomy (Skill Wiki).
- Covers seven distinct creative software domains with consistent structure and terminology.
- Employs LLM-driven, multimodal parsing to normalize unstructured resources.
- Enables applications in curriculum design, skill assessment, and intelligent assistants.
- Provides resources and evaluation demonstrating coverage and accuracy.
