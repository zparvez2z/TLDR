# Multi-Agent Spatial Transformer for Informed Map Construction and Structured Planning

This paper introduces a novel Multi-Agent Spatial Transformer (MAST) architecture designed for effectively integrating multi-agent partial observations into a shared spatial representation. The model improves informed map construction and supports structured planning in multi-agent systems. The approach leverages attention mechanisms to align and fuse spatial information from multiple agents, enabling enhanced decision-making capabilities in complex environments. Experimental results demonstrate MAST's effectiveness in tasks requiring cooperative map-building and navigation compared to baseline methods.
