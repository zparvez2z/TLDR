# A Large-Scale Study on the Development and Issues of Multi-Agent AI Systems

This paper conducts an extensive study on multi-agent AI systems, analyzing their development processes and common challenges. It covers a broad dataset collected from real-world multi-agent environments and investigates issues related to coordination, scalability, and system robustness. The study also reviews related work, documents the methodology used for data collection and preprocessing, and presents key findings on the status and difficulties faced in multi-agent AI research. The paper aims to provide insights to guide future advancements in this area. Key takeaways include the complexity of multi-agent coordination, the critical role of dataset quality, and the need for improved debugging and evaluation techniques.
